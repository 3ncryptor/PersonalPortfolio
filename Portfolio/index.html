About Me

I am Aryan Vibhuti, a passionate Ethical Hacker and Developer. My journey started with coding at an early age, and today I specialize in Penetration Testing and Vulnerability Assessment. Currently, I am a Penetration Tester intern at WIPRO, working on security projects aimed at protecting critical systems from cyber-attacks.

Besides ethical hacking, I am proficient in game development, video editing, and audio engineering. I have also completed freelance projects in the VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing) space. My creative and technical skillset spans across multiple domains, making me a well-rounded professional.
